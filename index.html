About me

I'm a QA Engineer with over 6 years of experience in FinTech and enterprise environments, combining deep software testing expertise with a strong analytical background in data validation and SQL-based analysis.

My approach to quality goes beyond test cases. I bring a data-driven mindset to every project. I use SQL and Power BI to analyse system behaviour, identify failure patterns, validate business logic, and measure the real impact of bugs before and after release.

I specialise in designing test strategies that cover both functional correctness and data integrity: ensuring that what the system does is right, and that the data it produces can be trusted. This dual focus makes me effective in environments where data quality and software reliability are equally critical — from automated pipelines to real-time reporting systems.
